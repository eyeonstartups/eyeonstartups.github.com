So this GITR thing, what is all the fuzz about?

worldwide presence

GITR is the world's largest pitching competition for start-ups currently hosted in over 60 countries.

From local to global

The UK national winner will go through to the Western European finals and the winner of this final will receive an entry ticket to the global GITR final.

A podium to perform and to build a fanbase

GITR offers promising start-ups a podium to build a global fan base. Start-ups battle head to head in a real boxing ring, in front of a panel of professional judges and a mixed audience of potential investors, partners, clients, users and fans.

Unique pitching concept

The uniquenes of the GITR concept lies in the features of the live-event: interactive, fun, engaging, inspiring and competitive.

Innovative structure

GITR distinguishes itself from other pitching events by its international multi-round structure, and valuable pitch preparation sessions for all participants.

Eligibility

UK start-ups are eligible to join the competition if they:

- Exist no longer than 5 years

- Have a scalable and innovative business model, and

- Are actively looking to raise seed or early-stage growth financing

The Battles

Each battle consists of 5 rounds:

- Round 1: Team

- Round 2: Achievements

- Round 3: Business model and market

- Round 4: Financials and investment proposition

- Round 5: Freestyle
