About us

Eyeonstartups is the UK host of Get In The Ring.

Our aim is to offer the most promising UK start-ups the chance to share their message with the rest of the world!

The Team

We are an eclectic team of passionate and ambitious people (5 at the moment) with backgrounds in events, finance, running a start-up and programming.

Amongst the team we have 3 nationalities (Dutch, German and English), we speak 10 languages, have started 4 companies and have lived in 10 countries.

A truly global oriented team ready to be the UK host of a truly global pitching competition!
