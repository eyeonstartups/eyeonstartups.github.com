So this GITR thing, what is all the fuzz about?

worldwide presence

GITR is the world's largest pitching competition for start-ups currently hosted in ca. 100 countries

From local to global

Country winners go through to the regional finals, and all regional winner receive an entry ticket to the annual global final.

Live battles

Start-ups battle head to head in a real boxing ring, in front of a panel of professional judges and an audience of investors and potential clients, users and fans.

Dynamic format

GITR battles are fast-paced, challenging and entertaining

Innovative structure

GITR distinguishes itself from other pitching events by its international multi-round structure, and valuable pitch preparation sessions for all participants.

Eligibility

UK start-ups are eligible to join the competition if they:

- Exist no longer than 5 years

- Have a scalable and innovative business model, and

- Are actively looking to raise seed or early-stage growth financing
